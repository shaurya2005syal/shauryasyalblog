The new Delhi bill 2021 | LG now the head of Delhi.

Apr 07, 2021






This new bill was recently passed by the parliament.  It gives more powers to the Delhi LG. Than the elected government. The chief minister of Delhi (Arvind Kejriwal ) has termed this an unconstitutional law. This bill was recently passed in Rajya Sabha by voice vote. many opposition MPs protested against the bill but it was passed comfortably.  Many MPs walked out of the parliament. 


this bill states that the government in Delhi means the lieutenant governor. This also states that any administrative decision taken by the Delhi elected government now needs to be approved by the LG.  this practically takes away all the powers of the CM and gives them to the LG.
